Welcome to Wamunyiri Football Club

Wamunyiri Football Club is a professional football club based in Western, Kenya, that competes in regional leagues. Founded in 1960, the club's home ground is the Wamunyiri Primary School grounds. We've been playing there since the foundation of the club. Wamunyiri F.C. adopted a red and green jersey, and over the years has won major league trophies.

  • Current Manager: Denis
  • Stadium: Wamunyiri Primary Field
  • Location: Western, Kenya
  • Trophies: 5 Regional Championships

The club is communally owned by the 'Westerners' and financially supported by prominent leaders in the Western Region. Our teams consist of several age groups: Under 14 boys, Under 17 boys, girls team, and the main team. The main team includes villagers, university students, and high school students, while the youth teams nurture future talent.

Prime Team

This was the first squad which brought a feeling of success to the team and the whole community. They played many matches and secured crucial wins, becoming one of the most feared teams during the 2010s era. Most players are now retired but still offer support to the team.

Our Sponsors

The team benefits from dedicated sponsors who stand by us in thick and thin. They provide training kits, medical equipment, and playing facilities - crucial for the sustainability of the team.

Misanya has been with the team since the 2010s. She is well respected and loved by everyone in the community. The first image shows Hum, Misanya and Alan - veteran players who were part of the prime team that started an era at the club.

Some sponsors also participate in training sessions and matches. Paulo is one of them, having been with the team since its foundation.

Academy

We nurture passionate kids from a young age to develop the team's traditions and make it easier to adapt to team tactics. Many have graduated from the academy and succeeded in the main team, like Nyanja. This encourages more children to join as they aspire to follow in their footsteps.

Training is often done together with the main team, which helps the academy players become tough, persistent, and gain experience from our senior players. The kids have competed in many competitions with excellent performances and results, recently finishing second in regional competitions.

Nyanja (bottom picture, last line extreme right) has become an important player for the main team. The coach trusts him and utilizes him in various positions due to his versatility and experience.

Player Spotlights

James (Jimmy), at the far left in the team photos, has been with the team since its early days. A versatile player who contributes in defense, midfield, and attack, he's currently a student at Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology.

Mike (wearing short #8) progressed from our youth team to become our main box-to-box midfielder. He's currently furthering his education at college while playing for the team.

Sany joined the team at a young age and has been loyal ever since. Despite his size, he's a natural defender - perhaps the most comfortable and composed defender on the ball in the whole team. Coming from a sporting family (his father was a player and now a referee), Sany has also played upfront thanks to his ball control and finishing ability.
