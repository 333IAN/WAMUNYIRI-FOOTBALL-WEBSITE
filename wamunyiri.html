Wamunyiri Football Club

Wamunyiri Football Club is a proffessional football club based in Western,Kenya,that competes in the regionals leagues.Founded in 1960.The club's homeground is the Wamunyiri Primary School grounds.They have been playing there since the foundation of the club.Wamunyiri F.C adopted a red and green jersey.Over the course of it's existence the club won major league trophies in their honour.

  • Current Manager: Denis
  • Stadium: Wamunyiri Primary Field
  • Location: Western,Kenya

The club is communally owned by the 'Westerners' and financially supported by most prominent leaders in the Western Region.The team cosists of several age groups as we shall see below.Age groups begin from Under 14 boys, Under 17 boys,girls team and the main team.The main team mostly consists of villagers,university students and high school students.Under 17 team is usually filled with primamry school kids. Under 14 team consists of lower primary children.
